THE HEALTH AND WELLNESS PLATFORM

Slowly, gently, we invite you to join us on a serene odyssey towards optimum health and wellness. Under the calming shade of our project's title, we explore the tranquil art of well-being, savoring each step of the journey. Embrace the unhurried pace, as we unravel the secrets to a balanced and harmonious life. Welcome to a sanctuary of serenity, where your path to holistic health begins, one peaceful moment at a time.

WEIGHT CONTROL 30 days plan

DAY 1

Day 1 Breakfast – One cup oatmeal with half cup non-fat milk, one tablespoon honey and a cup of berries Lunch – Two slices of whole-grain bread with sliced chicken breast with tomato slices and lettuce Snack – One apple with a handful of nuts such as almonds and walnuts Dinner – One cup bean soup with a bowl of green salad and grilled fish or chicken with lemon juice dressing

Day 2 Breakfast – Besan pancakes with mint chutney Lunch – Two cups Mexican black bean salad Snack – One cup of blueberries and green tea Dinner – Chicken and broccoli pasta and a side serving of salad with olive oil or vinegar dressing

Day 3 Breakfast – Three scrambled egg whites with a whole-grain toast Lunch – A whole-wheat wrap with lettuce and tofu with honey mustard dressing Snack – One diced cucumber with half a cup of shelled edamame tossed in lemon juice Dinner – One cup cooked quinoa with sauteed veggies such as carrots, broccoli and cherry tomatoes
