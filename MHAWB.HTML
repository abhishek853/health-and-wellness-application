DAY 1

Day 1: Make a gratitude list. Write down everything you're grateful for and why. Don't just write, "My family." Think about the ways in which your family has helped you become who you are, and how they support you and make you feel loved. You can even include things like, "The sun," or "My dog."

DAY 3
